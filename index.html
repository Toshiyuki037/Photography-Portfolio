MAX MAEHARA

Landscape, Sports, and Portrait Photography

My name is Max and I am a photographer from Oahu, Hawaii, currently based between Oahu and Corvallis, Oregon while studying Honors Electrical and Computer Engineering at Oregon State University. This portfolio website was designed and built from scratch to showcase my photography.

I primarily shoot with a Sony A7RV using a 200-600mm, 70-200mm, and 35mm lens. My work covers sports, landscape, product, and portrait photography across both Hawaii and Oregon.
